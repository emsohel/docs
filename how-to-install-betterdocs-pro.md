<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How To Install BetterDocs PRO</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Make sure that you have <a href="http://betterdocs.co/docs/activate-betterdocs/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><strong>Installed and Activated the Free version of BetterDocs</strong></a>. You will need to keep the Free version activated to use the Premium Version.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":12418,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-full"><img src="http://wordpress.test/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/image-1-17.png" alt="Install BetterDocs PRO" class="wp-image-12418" /></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Step 2:</strong> Go to your <a href="https://wpdeveloper.com/account/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><strong>WPDeveloper account</strong></a> and under the <strong>“Downloads” </strong>tab, you will see your downloadable files. Afterward, download the plugin file. It should look like <strong>betterdocs-pro.zip.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":12419,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-full"><img src="http://wordpress.test/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/image-1-18.png" alt="Install BetterDocs PRO" class="wp-image-12419" /></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Step 3:</strong> Afterward, Go to your <strong>Plugins</strong> page from <strong>WordPress Dashboard. </strong>Then click on the <strong>‘Add New’</strong> option and Upload the BetterDocs PRO plugin that you have downloaded. After successfully uploading, click on the <strong>‘Install Now’</strong> button.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":12420,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-full"><img src="http://wordpress.test/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/image-1-19.png" alt="Install BetterDocs PRO" class="wp-image-12420" /></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Step 4:</strong> After installation is finished, simply click on the <strong>‘Activate’</strong> button.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":12421,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-full"><img src="https://betterdocs.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/image-20.png" alt="Install BetterDocs PRO" class="wp-image-12421" title="How to Install BetterDocs PRO? 4" /></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>After activating the plugin, you will be redirected to the BetterDocs Settings page. From here, you can configure the basic settings of BetterDocs.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":12422,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-full"><img src="http://wordpress.test/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/image-29.gif" alt="Install BetterDocs PRO" class="wp-image-12422" /></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>After you have finished activation of <a href="http://betterdocs.co/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><strong>BetterDocs PRO</strong></a>, This is how your Plugins page will look like:</p>
